SERVICES:

Coffee shops offer a variety of coffee drinks,
including organic, fair-trade,
or in-house roasted coffee.

Coffee shops offer a variety of tea options.
There are many different types of tea; some have a cooling,
slightly bitter, and astringent flavour, while others
have profiles that include sweet, nutty,
floral, or grassy notes.

Coffee shops may serve pastries,
baked goods, salads, and sandwiches.
Common pastry dishes include pies, tarts,
quiches, croissants, and pasties.
